The Firearms Buyback Program portal is a secure online portal where you can:

  • submit a firearm compensation claim
  • manage your firearm shipments and disposal options
  • get updates on the status of your claim

Choose one of the following options:

Sign in or register with GCKey

GCKey gives you secure access to Government of Canada online services from different departments.

If you don't already have one, you may register for one.

Sign in with a secure online banking partner

This options allows you to use the same sign-in information you already use for online banking.

None of your financial or banking information will be shared with Public Safety. Your sign-in partners will not know which government service you are using

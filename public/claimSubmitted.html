What happens next

Shipping your firearms

We will contact you within the next few days to arrange shipping for the firearms indicated on the claim to a designated site in accordance with Storage, Display and Transportation of Firearms and Other Weapons by Businesses Regulations (SOR/98-210)This link will open in a new window under the Firearms Act.

Once the firearms are received and verified, your claim will be finalized and payment issued.

Deactivating your firearms

If you will be deactivating any of these firearms, you will need to complete a firearm deactivation form and bring it to a licensed gunsmith.

Once we receive proof of deactivation, your claim will be finalized and payment issued.

Learn more about deactivating your firearms.

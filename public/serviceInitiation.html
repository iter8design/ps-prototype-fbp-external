Eligibility

Any owner of a prohibited firearm that is eligible for compensation.

What you need before you start

It should take 15-20 minutes to submit your claim depending on the number of firearms you have. To begin, you will need the following information:

  • list of all registered firearms you have including:
    • firearm reference number (FRN), make, model, serial number
  • list of all unregistered firearms you have including:
    • firearm reference number (FRN), make, model serial number, capacity/shots, calibre,
